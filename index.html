Robot


Designed and built a robot using Arduino to take an order, follow a line and deliver food to customers, essentially a robotic waiter for restaurants.



×

Robot

Photo of the Robot.

One of the projects that I have worked on at Northeastern was an autonomous waiter robot that could take an order from a customer, report that order to the kitchen and return to the table with the food on it. I did most of the coding, implementing a two Arduino system where one acted as the menu and the other as the driver, I created a fully interactive menu/order system and a line tracking program that allows the robot to follow any dark tape line and stop at the end. I also helped in the design process, creating models for the drive base, the top platter of the robot, and a few small brackets/holders that were later printed for the robot, this was done using SolidWorks. Through this process I learned a lot about the engineering design process and how to properly work around constraints and issues that present themselves. I also got valuable coding experience and SolidWorks experience.
